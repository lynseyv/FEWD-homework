  About Us

Hi. I'm Matt. I like to make things.

If you're in any way familiar with popular media — such as the Internet — you've probably noticed that the demand for high-quality video and audio has recently skyrocketed. Thousands of business, organizations and individuals have found themselves in a world where a multimedia presence is a necessity.

Fortuitously, we also live in a world where the cost of high-quality equipment has drastically decreased. DSLRs now allow millions of filmmakers a level of production value that was completely out of reach of the average person 10 years ago. Home recording studios and project studios have become commonplace for budding producers and musicians. Indeed, with the proper skill, one can produce stunning pieces of media at a fraction of the traditional cost.

So that's what I'm offering you: the right equipment with the right skills. I've been working with audio and video both professionally and academically for more than 10 years. I want to share what I've learned with you, and make some really amazing stuff. Looking forward to hearing from you.

Services

We offer a variety of services to meet your needs. We are flexible and open to negotiating many different types of projects.

  Digital Video

Stunning HD video, captured with high quality lenses and powerful camera bodies. A variety of rigging equipment allows us to shoot in virtually any scenario.

  Audio Capture

​

Exceptional audio recording using broadcast and studio standard microphones and professional-level recording devices. All audio captured at 48khz, 24-bit, for optimal fidelity and usability.

  Editing

Post-production and editing, fine-tuned to your needs, using Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, Motion, Photoshop and other industry standard editing suites.
